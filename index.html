FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers before you get started.

How do I get a key? +
Click "Get My Key", enter your Minecraft username and complete 4 short steps. Your key is generated instantly and lasts 24 hours.
How do I activate the mod? +
Launch Minecraft with the mod installed. An activation screen appears automatically. Paste your key and hit Activate.
My key expired. What do I do? +
Go through the steps again to get a fresh 24-hour key. It's free every time.
Does this work on all servers? +
It works on most servers. Use responsibly and at your own risk.
What Minecraft version? +
Fabric 1.21.5. More versions may be added later.
