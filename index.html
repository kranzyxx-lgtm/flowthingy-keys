FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How do I get a key?
Click "Get My Key" at the top, enter your Minecraft username and complete 4 short steps. Your key is generated instantly and is valid for 24 hours.
How do I activate the mod?
Launch Minecraft with the mod installed. An activation screen will appear automatically. Paste your key and click Activate.
My key expired. What do I do?
Simply go through the key steps again to generate a new 24-hour key. It's free every time.
Does this work on all Minecraft servers?
It works on most servers. Use responsibly and at your own risk.
What Minecraft version is supported?
Currently supports Fabric 1.21.5. More versions may be added in the future.
